# **The KJGV Bible**  
*A programmatic, **as‑literal‑as‑it‑gets** translation of the Westminster Leningrad Codex*

## **Overview**
The **KJGV Bible** is a 39‑volume translation of the Hebrew Scriptures (Tanakh), built directly from the Westminster Leningrad Codex. This repository contains the complete text of **Genesis**, presented with full morphological transparency and a consistent, rule‑driven methodology.

## **Features**
- **Programmatic translation**  
  A rule‑driven system that reflects the actual tense, mood, voice, and morphology of each Hebrew form.

- **Root‑faithful English renderings**  
  Each Hebrew root is translated with a single, consistent English meaning across all contexts.

- **Bold‑root highlighting**  
  Hebrew roots are visually emphasized within each word to reveal patterns and relationships.

- **Standardized Masoretic‑based vocalization**  
  Nikkudot are retained and applied consistently; cantillation marks are removed.

- **Morphological transparency**  
  Part of speech, gender, number, and person are explicitly represented in the English output.

- **Hebraic‑perspective**  
  Preserves the form and the way ancient Hebrews expressed themselves — **the structure, the worldview, the meaning**.

- **Extensive glossaries**  
  Includes both Hebrew→English and English→Hebrew glossaries.

## **Design Tradeoffs**
This translation prioritizes structural transparency over idiomatic smoothing. As a result, the English may feel unfamiliar at times. This is intentional: the goal is to reveal the internal logic of Biblical Hebrew.

## **Purpose**
Ideal for:
- Readers seeking a Hebraic‑perspective translation  
- Students learning Biblical Hebrew  
- Linguists and researchers  
- Software developers working with biblical text
