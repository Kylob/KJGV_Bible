# **The KJGV Bible**  
*A programmatic, **as‑literal‑as‑it‑gets** translation of the Westminster Leningrad Codex*

## **Overview**
**The KJGV Bible** is a multi‑volume, programmatically generated translation of the Hebrew Scriptures (Tanakh), built directly from the Westminster Leningrad Codex.

This repository contains the complete text of:
- **Genesis** (bereshit) in beginning
- **Job** (iyov) be hostile

Presented with full morphological transparency and a consistent, rule‑driven methodology.

## **Purpose**
The purpose of this translation is to present the Hebrew text of the Westminster Leningrad Codex in an English form that preserves its structure, reflects its lexical precision, and exposes its underlying patterns without interpretive smoothing or doctrinal framing.

## **Features**
- **Programmatic translation**  
  A rule‑driven system that accurately reflects the tense, mood, voice, and morphology of each Hebrew form.

- **Root‑faithful English renderings**  
  Every Hebrew word is translated with a consistent English meaning that remains faithful to its underlying root across all contexts.

- **Bold‑root highlighting**  
  Hebrew roots are visually emphasized within each word to reveal patterns and relationships.

- **Standardized Masoretic‑based vocalization**  
  Nikkudot are retained and applied consistently, while cantillation marks are removed.

- **Morphological transparency**  
  Part of speech, gender, number, person, and verbal nuances are consistently expressed in the English rendering.

- **Hebraic‑perspective**  
  The form of the Hebrew text, as used by the ancient Hebrew authors, is transparently preserved.

- **Comprehensive concordances**  
  Both Hebrew→English and English→Hebrew translation concordances are included to trace and compare how meanings have been rendered throughout the text.

## **Philosophy**
- **Accuracy**  
  This translation reflects the precise grammatical and lexical information present in the Hebrew text without adding interpretive smoothing or doctrinal framing.
- **Consistency**  
  Each Hebrew root is mapped to a stable English rendering, allowing readers to follow meaning across contexts without contextual reinterpretation.
- **Transparency**  
  All translation decisions are exposed through structural fidelity, morphological clarity, and concordances that show how meanings have been rendered throughout the text.

## **Usage**
This translation enables you to:
- study word usage with morphological clarity
- engage the text with with a fresh, unfiltered perspective
- follow meanings across contexts without interpretive paraphrasing
